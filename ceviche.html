Delicious Colombian ceviche recipe

Hello, flavor lovers!

Get ready for a culinary journey to Colombia, where the freshness of the sea merges with the explosion of flavors of Colombian cuisine. Today, we will teach you how to prepare Colombian ceviche, a dish that delights with its refreshing acidity, crunchy texture, and vibrant flavors.

This ceviche is not just a dish; it is a tradition enjoyed on the Colombian coasts, a symbol of joy and life on the Caribbean coast.

Are you ready to dive into this culinary experience? Join us and discover the secret of Colombian ceviche!

And what are the ingredients?

  • 1 cup of fresh shrimp.
  • 1 onion and garlic.
  • 1 cup of cilantro.
  • Seedless lemon juice to avoid bitterness.
  • Tomato sauce, chili, and salt.

Let's go straight to the preparation.

  1. Boil water with salt until it reaches a boiling point, then add the shrimp.
  2. Let it sit for about two minutes.
  3. Drain the shrimp while adding the onion, garlic, cilantro, and finally, of course, the shrimp, lemon juice, and tomato sauce in a glass bowl.
  4. Add salt and chili to taste. Stir.
  5. Serve in a short glass for ceviche.
  6. It is recommended to soak the onion in salt for about 10 minutes to reduce its flavor. After that, wash it very well with enough water so it doesn't remain salty. Also, it is advised not to overcook the shrimp because if they are overcooked, they will become tough.

Congratulations! Now you have in your hands a delicious Colombian ceviche, ready to enjoy.

Remember that this dish is an explosion of flavors and textures, so savor every bite. You can accompany your ceviche with patacones, arepas, or yuca for an even more complete experience.

Share this recipe with your friends and family and let them discover the magic of Colombian ceviche. Enjoy!
